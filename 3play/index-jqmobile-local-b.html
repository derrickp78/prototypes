HOW TO PLAY

GET TODAY'S CARDS

You will will be dealt five cards. Each card will have a prediction of a team or player performance for their game today.

MAKE YOUR PICKS

To make a pick, you will select if the actual outcome of the prediction will be more or less. Your pick will drop into one of the three slots.

CHOOSE WISELY

You can only make three predictions to complete your 3Play, so choose wisely. If you've already made three picks and change your mind, you can swap out a pick.

SUBMIT YOUR PICKS

After you have made your three picks, tap "Submit" to complete your entry. You can change your picks anytime until the games start.
